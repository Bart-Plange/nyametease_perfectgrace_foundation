NYAMETEASE PERFECT GRACE Foundation

Nyametease Perfect Grace Foundation is a non-profitable, non-political and a Ghana-based organization started many years ago. The Foundation aims to sponsor and encourage education for all, especially children. In the light of this, it targets children from all ethnic backgrounds and race and gears them towards opportunities.


The foundation is currently focused on feeding and providing school fees to the needy and valnerable families.

Nyametease Perfect Grace Foundation seeks to provide the needs of children from broken homes, dropouts and children without the hope of tomorrow to a place to find grace.
