Sample Questions That May be Needful

What is the mission of the foundation?

The foundation is dedicated to improving the lives of the poor and the needy by providing access to education, healthcare, and essential support. We are committed to breaking the cycle of poverty by helping to pay school fees, healthcare bills and offering maximum support to those in need.

Is it possible to donate from every nation or state?

Absolutely yes! Nyametease Perfect Grace foundation has the vision to improve lives and to affect the hopeless positively. It has made it possible to donate from everywhere all around the world

You can donate through a bank or use the MTN mobile money shortcode provided on the website.

Can I volunteer to be a part of the foundation?

We welcome volunteers who share the passion for making a positive impact on the world. kindly send us a message or call directly to know how to easily get involved.

Can I partner with the organization?

Absolutely, you can partner with us from anywhere around the world. we are looking forward to individuals and organizations that share a common interest with us.

Kindly send us a message or call now to know more about how to partner with us to achieve a common goal of securing and impacting lives.
